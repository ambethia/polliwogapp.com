Polliwog

The app error app iPhone app.

Polliwog is an iPhone client for the Hoptoad API. Hoptoad is a service that collects errors generated by other web apps.
With Polliwog, you can:

  • Browse resolved and unresolved errors for all your projects.
  • Inspect details about your exceptions, including request parameters, session data and backtraces.
  • Resolve errors right from your iPhone.
